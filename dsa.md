# 🧠 Data Structures & Algorithms Roadmap

> **Timeline:** 3–5 months | **Level:** Beginner to Intermediate

---

## Phase 1 — Basics (Month 1)

### Programming Fundamentals
- Pick a language: Python or Java recommended
- Time & space complexity, Big O notation
- ⏱️ Timeline: 1 week
- 📚 Resources:
  - [Big O Cheat Sheet](https://www.bigocheatsheet.com)
  - [CS50 (free)](https://cs50.harvard.edu)

### Arrays & Strings
- Traversal, two pointers, sliding window
- ⏱️ Timeline: 2 weeks
- 📚 Resources:
  - [LeetCode Arrays](https://leetcode.com/tag/array/)
  - [NeetCode YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@NeetCode)

---

## Phase 2 — Core Data Structures (Month 2–3)

### Linked Lists
- Singly, doubly, circular
- Reversal, cycle detection (Floyd's algorithm)
- ⏱️ Timeline: 1 week

### Stacks & Queues
- Implementation, monotonic stack
- ⏱️ Timeline: 1 week

### Trees & Graphs
- Binary trees, BST, DFS, BFS
- Graph traversal, topological sort
- ⏱️ Timeline: 3 weeks
- 📚 Resources:
  - [Visualgo](https://visualgo.net)
  - [William Fiset Graph Theory](https://www.youtube.com/@WilliamFiset-videos)

### Hashing
- HashMap, HashSet, frequency counting
- ⏱️ Timeline: 1 week

---

## Phase 3 — Algorithms (Month 3–4)

### Sorting & Searching
- Bubble, merge, quick sort
- Binary search & variations
- ⏱️ Timeline: 2 weeks

### Dynamic Programming
- Memoization, tabulation
- Classic problems: knapsack, LCS, coin change
- ⏱️ Timeline: 3 weeks
- 📚 Resources:
  - [DP Patterns by Aladdin](https://leetcode.com/discuss/general-discussion/458695)
  - [Striver DP Series](https://www.youtube.com/@takeUforward)

### Greedy & Backtracking
- ⏱️ Timeline: 2 weeks
